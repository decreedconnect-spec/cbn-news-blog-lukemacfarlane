VOGUE SLOVENIJA Features LUKE MACFARLANE

"Modeling for Vogue in Slovenia for the first time"

  • Rising star in acting and modeling
  • Celebrated LGBTQ+ advocate
  • Returning to his European roots

Luke Macfarlane graces the April 2026 cover of Vogue Slovenija, marking a historic first for the Canadian-American actor and LGBTQ+ icon. Shot on location in Ljubljana, the editorial showcases Macfarlane's effortless transition from Hollywood to the high-fashion world.
